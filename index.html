about

We're a small team passionate about artificial intelligence and great user experience. Founded by developers and designers who care deeply about quality, we believe AI should enhance human capabilities, not replace them.

Every product we create goes through rigorous testing and iteration. We listen to our users, learn from their feedback, and continuously improve. Our approach is simple: build tools we'd want to use ourselves.

Based remotely, we work with talented people around the world who share our vision of making AI accessible and useful for everyone.
